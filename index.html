INSTRUCTION


To use this website, you will control your avatar using the "W", "S", "A", and "D" keys on your keyboard. Your avatar can be directed to move into specific departments where you can witness how computer science and technology have played a vital role in medical advancements.
Explore the Departments:

Key Action
W Move forward
S Move backward
A Move left
D Move right
E Interact

Discover Advancements:

As you navigate your avatar into different departments, you will encounter nodes representing specific equipment or features. These nodes showcase how computer science has contributed to the advancement of medical practices.

Access Information:

Once you have reached the desired location with your avatar, you can access detailed information about the specific department or equipment you are interested in by pressing the 'E' key.
Enjoy your journey through the interconnected world of technology and medicine at Tile Hospital!
